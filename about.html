Mrs. Kruer

I am looking forward to getting to know your child, but first I would like to tell you a little about me. This is my fourth year teaching at Greenville Elementary. I also worked at this school previously as a teacher's aide and a substitute teacher. My four children also attended Greenville. I have been a member of the Greenville Familly for a long time!

My family lives on a small farm in Greenville. My husband is a surveyor, but he would love to be a farmer! We bale hay, raise cows and chickens. We also have a donkey, goats, cats and a new puppy. My children are very involved in $-H. My oldest Carly is 20 and works at a nursing home in Corydon. Payton attends the University of Louisville. Nick is a junior at Floyd Central.

We had an extremely busy summer with 4-H. In July my family hosted a 4-H exchange student from Poland. We loved having Agnieska be a part of our family. It was very interesting to learn about her life in Poland.
